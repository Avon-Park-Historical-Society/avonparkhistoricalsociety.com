# Support the Sebring Historical Society

## Your donation makes a difference

You can choose to make a monetary donation or a memorial donation in loving memory of someone special.

Your donation helps preserve Sebring’s history and supports the ongoing work of the Sebring Historical Society. Every contribution helps us protect historical records, artifacts, and stories that keep our community’s past alive. Donations of any amount make a difference.

A memorial donation is a meaningful way to honor someone’s life while helping preserve Sebring’s history for future generations. Gifts can be made in loving memory of any individual, or in memory of Jim Pollard, former president of the Sebring Historical Society, whose dedication and passion for preserving our city’s heritage continue to inspire our work today. Every contribution made in remembrance helps keep Sebring’s story alive.
