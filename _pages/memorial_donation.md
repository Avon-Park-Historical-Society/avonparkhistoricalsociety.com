# Memorial Donation

## Donations in memory of

A memorial donation is a meaningful way to honor someone’s life while helping preserve Sebring’s history for future generations. Gifts can be made in loving memory of any individual, or in memory of Jim Pollard, former president of the Sebring Historical Society, whose dedication and passion for preserving our city’s heritage continue to inspire our work today. Every contribution made in remembrance helps keep Sebring’s story alive.
